Who am I ?

Greetings! I'm delighted to share a bit about myself with you. I'm Beraat, a dedicated individual with a diverse range of passions and an ongoing journey of learning. I am currently pursuing my studies in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Hasan Kalyoncu University. As a student in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering,

I am immersed in the captivating world of circuits, electronics, and technology. The intricate workings of electrical systems and the marvels of electronic devices continue to fascinate me and drive my curiosity to explore new horizons within this field. However, amidst my academic pursuits, my heart beats strongly for the realm of software and programming.


The logic and creativity behind coding, the ability to build digital solutions from scratch, and the endless potential to create meaningful applications captivate my imagination. Despite my passion for engineering, my affinity for software development has grown stronger over time. I find the process of coding to be akin to a form of artistic expression, where I can bring ideas to life through lines of code and algorithms. Beyond academics, I find solace and joy in the world of music. Crafting melodies, experimenting with harmonies, and expressing emotions through music is an essential part of who I am. This creative outlet complements my technical pursuits and adds a well-rounded dimension to my life. In addition to music and technology, I am an avid enthusiast of electronic devices. Understanding their inner workings and discovering ways to enhance their functionalities is a hobby I hold dear. This curiosity naturally aligns with my passion for software and engineering, as they all involve problem-solving and innovation.

A significant part of my university journey involves my involvement as a co-founder in the "Mirketgiller" student community. Collaborating with like-minded peers to organize events, share insights, and foster a sense of belonging has been a rewarding experience that enriches my time at the university. While I am still in the process of completing my studies, I have come to realize that my heart leans more towards the world of software development. The process of translating ideas into functional applications and the ever-evolving landscape of technology keep me excited and motivated to continually learn and improve. Outside of my academic pursuits, I find enjoyment in the realm of computer games. The strategic challenges and immersive narratives of games provide a dynamic escape that complements my analytical and creative thinking. In summary, my journey is one of continuous growth, exploration, and adaptation. As I navigate through my studies at Hasan Kalyoncu University, I am driven by my passion for software, my fascination with electronics, my love for music, and my commitment to fostering connections within the student community. Through these endeavors, I am shaping an identity that encompasses my multifaceted interests and aspirations.
